    Chapter 4 Probability

    In this chapter we will investigate some basic probability concepts. We will begin with a brief review of what probability is, how to use a tree diagram to determine the number outcomes, and discuss common rules for compound events. We will extend this to discrete probability distributions.

    Objectives

    At the end of this chapter, you will be able to:
    1. Calculate the probability of simple events.
    2. Use the probability rules for OR, AND, GIVEN statements.
    3. Determine conditional probability.
    4. Use a tree diagram to determine the probability for multiple events.
    5. Calculate permutations and combinations.
    6. Determine the likelyhood of events from a probability distribution.
    7. Evaluate a binomial distribution.
